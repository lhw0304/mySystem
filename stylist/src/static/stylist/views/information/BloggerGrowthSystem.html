MODE Blogger Growth System

MODE Team Mar 2016

1. MODE Blogger Growth System has totally 6 levels for bloggers to grow. After finishing tasks in correspondent level each month, the blogger will enter next level automatically. If the blogger fail to finish his/her tasks by 3 consecutive months, he/she shall be sent to a lower level.

2. Bloggers’ initial level may be different depending on their current followers, the quality of their posts and their influence on social media. MODE will inform the bloggers their starting level before the cooperation.

3. VIP bloggers will directly be classified on Level 6 in MODE system and MODE will help to create a personal brand and a different growing system for him/her specifically: he/she will be the brand owner to decide the design, production and operation ways with the support from a professional team. The blogger will have personal brands named after him/her, and will receive very attractive commission from 20%-30% of total sales.

4. All items sent by MODE should be exposed in MODE platform posts with purchasing links included.
